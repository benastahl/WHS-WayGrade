GPA Calculation
WayGrade's GPA viewing feature is calculated carefully and as accurate as possible, considering Wayland does not offer weighted GPAs anymore. We look for keywords "AP", "Applied Placement", and "Honors" to calculate weighted GPAs. Any class name that doesn't contain those keywords is weighted as a college class. Please note that the weighted GPA shown is never 100% accurate as colleges/universities have their own way of calculating admissions weighted gpa.
